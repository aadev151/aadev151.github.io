About my projects ▼
I strive to use programming as a tool to better the planet. Whether helping nonprofits, reducing plastic waste, or simply putting smiles on people's faces, I believe that coding can be really beneficial.
As a programmer, I adore Python, but also code in Swift, HTML&CSS&JS, and c++. I am not yet sure about who I want to become, but most of my projects are concenred with full-stack development.

HelpUnicorn

https://helpunicorn.pythonanywhere.com

An online resource uniting IT-specialists and nonprofits thus securing charities with free IT assistance. The ecosystem includes a website, a website builder for welfare funds, and beta versions of mobile apps. It now has over 1000 users, and my team and I keep promoting it in Russia, Belarus, and the US.

Project Neighbors

https://neighbors.pythonanywhere.com

A "swapper" social network for neighbor. I made it in 2020 as the final project for the Silicon Valley entrepreneurship workshop. Its idea is to encourage communication among neighbors through "swapping" things. F.i., I can share my Monopoly with you in excange for a cup of boiled coffee (I don't have a coffee machine)

Towelly

http://towellyapp.cf

In the hotel industry, there is a huge amount of unnesecary plastic that could be easily eliminated. For instance, each guest usually has at least two plastic cards - to open the room and to get a beach towel. Towelly is an app that replaces the second card using QR codes. It's a cheap and green alternative for hotels.

Duckystyle

https://github.com/aadev151/duckystyle

My Flask project for the Yandex workshop. I also made it together with Darya Eremeeva. It is a mock auction of DFTs (Duck Functional Tokens) - here you may buy and sell pictures of ducks, both with real money and "website coins". Food Buzzer

In the fast food industry, recently hyping food buzzers are an extremely cheap thing, but they are made of plastic. I want to create an app capable of replacing them. Using it, clients will create an anonymous chat with the restaurant, which will be deleted as soon as their order is taken. This will help fight the plastic pollution.

Online chat with urologists / gynecologists

Any person may feel awkward and uncomfortable going to such doctors. It might be a great challenge to get over oneself and tell about intimate problems to an unknown person. With this chat, any person will be able to consult with specialists anonymously and see whether they have a serious health problem or it's just something they can solve themselves.
